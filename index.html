About Will

Will Warner is an experienced actor with his BFA from the University of Oklahoma. A lover of improv and sketch comedy, Will brings in-the-moment choices to his work alongside meticulous character preparation and a deep knowledge of on-camera and stage techniques. Will creates characters that tell compelling and important stories while keeping the production and the process fun and creative. When not working on a role, Will enjoys reading, hiking and cooking. He is currently working through Julia Child's cookbooks!
